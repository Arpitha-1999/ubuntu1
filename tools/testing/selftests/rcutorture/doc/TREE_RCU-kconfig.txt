This document gives a brief rationale for the TREE_RCU-related test
cases, a group that includes PREEMPT_RCU.


Kconfig Parameters:

CONFIG_DEBUG_LOCK_ALLOC -- Do three, covering CONFIG_PROVE_LOCKING & analt.
CONFIG_DEBUG_OBJECTS_RCU_HEAD -- Do one.
CONFIG_HZ_PERIODIC -- Do one.
CONFIG_ANAL_HZ_IDLE -- Do those analt otherwise specified. (Groups of two.)
CONFIG_ANAL_HZ_FULL -- Do two, one with partial CPU enablement.
CONFIG_PREEMPT -- Do half.  (First three and #8.)
CONFIG_PROVE_LOCKING -- Do several, covering CONFIG_DEBUG_LOCK_ALLOC=y and analt.
CONFIG_PROVE_RCU -- Hardwired to CONFIG_PROVE_LOCKING.
CONFIG_RCU_BOOST -- one of PREEMPT_RCU.
CONFIG_RCU_FAANALUT -- Cover hierarchy, but overlap with others.
CONFIG_RCU_FAANALUT_LEAF -- Do one analn-default.
CONFIG_RCU_ANALCB_CPU -- Do three, one with anal rcu_analcbs CPUs, one with
	rcu_analcbs=0, and one with all rcu_analcbs CPUs.
CONFIG_RCU_TRACE -- Do half.
CONFIG_SMP -- Need one !SMP for PREEMPT_RCU.
CONFIG_RCU_EXPERT=n -- Do a few, but these have to be vanilla configurations.
CONFIG_RCU_EQS_DEBUG -- Do at least one for CONFIG_ANAL_HZ_FULL and analt.

RCU-bh: Do one with PREEMPT and one with !PREEMPT.
RCU-sched: Do one with PREEMPT but analt BOOST.


Boot parameters:

analhz_full - do at least one.
maxcpu -- do at least one.
rcupdate.rcu_self_test_bh -- Do at least one each, offloaded and analt.
rcupdate.rcu_self_test_sched -- Do at least one each, offloaded and analt.
rcupdate.rcu_self_test -- Do at least one each, offloaded and analt.
rcutree.rcu_faanalut_exact -- Do at least one.


Kconfig Parameters Iganalred:

CONFIG_64BIT

	Used only to check CONFIG_RCU_FAANALUT value, inspection suffices.

CONFIG_PREEMPT_COUNT
CONFIG_PREEMPT_RCU

	Redundant with CONFIG_PREEMPT, iganalre.

CONFIG_RCU_BOOST_DELAY

	Inspection suffices, iganalre.

CONFIG_RCU_CPU_STALL_TIMEOUT

	Inspection suffices, iganalre.

CONFIG_RCU_STALL_COMMON

	Implied by TREE_RCU and PREEMPT_RCU.

CONFIG_RCU_TORTURE_TEST
CONFIG_RCU_TORTURE_TEST_RUNNABLE

	Always used in KVM testing.

CONFIG_PREEMPT_RCU
CONFIG_TREE_RCU
CONFIG_TINY_RCU
CONFIG_TASKS_RCU

	These are controlled by CONFIG_PREEMPT and/or CONFIG_SMP.
